Facts about Actor vijay

  • Vijay was born on 22 June 1974 as Joseph Vijay in Madras (now Chennai)
  • His father S. A. Chandrasekhar is a Tamil film director and his mother Shoba is a playback singer and carnatic vocalist.
  • Vijay married a fan of his,Sangeetha Sornalingam, a Hindu British Sri Lankan Tamil, on 25 August 1999 in both Hindu and Christian tradition.
  • Vijay started his acting career by playing a role in VETRI as a child.
  • Then he acted alongside with Vijayakanth in a movie directed by his father SAC.
  • Vijay begun to act as a hero from the movie Naalaya Theerpu for which he was mocked by the critics.
  • Earlier films of vijay did not do well at box-office.But he did not give up.
  • The first breakthrough film in Vijay's career was Poove Unakkaga.
  • Then he gave many hit films like Kadhalukku Mariyathai,Thullatha Manamum Thullum,Friends,Badhri,Youth etc..
  • Then ,Vijay acted in many action movies like Ghilli,Thirumala,Thiruppachi,Pokkiri..etc.
  • From 2007-11 he gave many flop movies like Sura,Vettaikkaran,ATM,Kuruvi.
  • Vijay gave his comeback by Thuppakki directed by A.R.Murugadoss
  • Now he is acting good movies like Kaththi,Mersal,Theri.
  • Vijay has faced many political issues in his life
  • Vijay has a big fan-base and an equal amount of Haters.
